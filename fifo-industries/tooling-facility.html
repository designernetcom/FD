Advanced Heavy-Duty Machining Series

Designed for high accuracy, reliability, and performance at higher speeds, this series features a high-dynamic structural design with rapid axis acceleration. The broad base and twin-layer heavy column significantly enhance vibration damping and maintain machine stability even during heavy-duty machining. A wide table supported by precision LM guideways ensures excellent load-carrying capacity. This series is ideal for high material removal in casting and forging operations, making it a perfect fit for die and mould applications.

Key Features:

Our Vision Measuring Machine (VMM) and Height Master systems are designed to deliver high-accuracy dimensional and visual inspection with consistent reliability

  • Broad column structure with robust construction
  • High load-bearing work table
  • Wider linear motion guideways for smooth and stable movement
  • Auto and manual coolant system

About FIFO Industries

FIFO Industries, established in June 2015 by Mr. D. B. Devaba, is driven by passion to serve the Plastic Injection Mould industry. With over a decade of expertise, we specialize in manufacturing a wide range of injection moulds including 2 Plate, 3 Plate, Unscrewing, and Hot Runner moulds. Our focus is on delivering top quality, cost optimization.
