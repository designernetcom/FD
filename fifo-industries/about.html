Certified Quality & Standards

FIFO Industries is an ISO 9001:2015 certified organization (certified by QRD), ensuring consistent quality, standardized processes, and continuous improvement across all manufacturing operations.

Advanced Manufacturing Expertise

We specialize in the manufacturing of high-precision plastic and rubber injection moulds, using premium-grade tool steels hardened through vacuum heat treatment technology to enhance tool life, accuracy, and performance.

Skilled Workforce & Infrastructure

Our operations are supported by a qualified and experienced team from reputed institutes. Critical machines are installed in a dust-free, controlled environment, enabling precision manufacturing and reliable output.

Industry-Focused & Globally Competitive

FIFO Industries delivers internationally competitive solutions in terms of quality, cost, lead time, and reliability, catering to industries such as Automotive, Electrical, Switchgear, Defence, and Toys across India.

Manufacturing Overview
